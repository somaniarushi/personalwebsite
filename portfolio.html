Current Projects

From teaching youngsters, to helping the elderly, to catalysing social conversation, to deliberating upon the ethical usage of cutting-edge software.

Sexual Assault Map

Rape and assaults are dangerously close to being normalised in Indian society.
To open a conversation about this problem, I'm working on an online map which would autonomously pinpoint the locations where assaults are being reported, to demonstrate how severe who problem actually is.

Old Age Home Letters

Eldery folk are often forgotten by society. I've recently realized how lonely they often get. I'm attempting to set up a portal which would allow people to send the eldery messages, which would be printed, read out or messaged over to them, as per their preference.

VR Drum Kit

Pushing the boundaries of music by implementing this instrument virtually through Oculus VR. Making the drums an instrument you can travel with, and making the drum an instrument anyone from any economic group can learn without financial hardship.

Avadh Media Manager

Managing the social media and reforming the website for Avadh Hospitals, Lucknow, to make it more interactive and optimise it. My intent is to make people more aware of the blood drives and camps the hospital does weekly.
